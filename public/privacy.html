Privacy Policy

Last updated July 2026

The Hourly is a private, invitation-only photo journal built for one family. This policy explains what information the app collects and how it's used. It is not intended for the general public, and access is limited to people the app's owner has personally invited.

What we collect

  • Account info: your name and email address, obtained either from Google Sign-In or the email you provide.
  • Activity: your timezone and the last time you were active in the app, used to show who's currently online.
  • Photos you upload: the image itself, and — only if your device grants location permission, or the photo's file contains embedded GPS data — the approximate city where it was taken, GPS coordinates, and local weather (temperature and humidity) at that location and time.
  • Comments and reactions: any comments or emoji reactions you leave on photos.

How it's used

All of the above is used only to run the app's core feature: letting invited family members share and view each other's hourly photos, see where and when they were taken, and comment or react. Nothing is used for advertising, analytics, or any purpose beyond the app itself.

Who can see it

Only people who have been individually invited to this family's private instance of the app can see any of this data. It is never sold, shared with third parties, or made public.

Google Sign-In

If you sign in with Google, we receive your name and email address from Google to create your account. We do not access, read, or post to any other Google data or service on your behalf.

Where data is stored

Data is stored using Supabase (a hosted database and file storage provider). Photos and account data are retained until you delete them.

Deleting your data

You can permanently delete your account, all your photos, and your comments and reactions at any time from the app's Settings screen ("Delete My Account & Photos"). This action is immediate and cannot be undone.

Children's privacy

The Hourly is intended for use by the invited family only and is not directed at children under 13 as a general product.
